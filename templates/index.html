How to Select Professional Resume

  • Choose a clean, simple layout without excessive graphics or colors.
  • Ensure contact information is prominently displayed at the top.
  • Include a clear, concise summary or objective statement.
  • Organize sections logically: Experience, Education, Skills, etc.
  • Use consistent, professional fonts and formatting.
  • Quantify achievements with specific metrics and examples.
  • Proofread thoroughly to avoid any typos or grammatical errors.
